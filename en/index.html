Jun Ding

Professor, Doctoral Supervisor
National Young Talents

Center For Alloy Innovation And Design

School Of Materials Science And Engineering

Xi'an Jiaotong University

Long-term commitment to the research of the structure-performance relationship of high-performance alloy materials and the mechanical deformation behavior of metal materials. To date, over 70 SCI papers have been published, with more than 6,000 citations; among them, important papers have been published as (co-)first author/corresponding author in journals such as Nature, Nature Materials (2 papers), PNAS (7 papers), Nature Communications (6 papers), Nano Letters, Acta Materialia, npj Computational Materials, etc. The research work has been selected as a research hotspot by the U.S. Department of Energy and reported by Phys.org. He/she leads national-level youth talent projects, general projects from the National Natural Science Foundation, youth projects, and Xi’an Jiaotong University’s outstanding young talent program.

Interests:

  • Disordered structural alloys: High-entropy alloys and Metallic glasses;

  • Accelerated computational materials discovery and design;

  • Additive Manufacturing of structural alloys;

  • Multiscale modelling of deformation of advanced structural alloys

  • Supercooled liquids and crystal-liquid interfaces
